Welcome to Shopify for ChatGPT

Automate your customer support with the power of AI

With Shopify for ChatGPT, say goodbye to the challenges of managing and responding to a high volume of support tickets. This platform integrates your Shopify orders and customer support queries coming via Gmail into one place. The power of GPT-4 AI helps you analyze queries, categorize them, and even auto-respond to common questions, freeing up your time to focus on growing your business. The seamless Gmail API integration ensures that all your customer communications remain up-to-date and easily accessible.

About Us

We are a dedicated team of developers and AI enthusiasts who believe in the power of technology to simplify the complexities of business operations. Our mission is to help businesses automate their customer support and streamline their order management using the power of ChatGPT.

Features

  • Order Integration: Synchronize your Shopify orders directly into ChatGPT.
  • Email Support: Link your Gmail account to manage support tickets in one place.
  • AI Assistance: Utilize the power of GPT-4 AI to automatically categorize and respond to customer queries.
