3. Use of APIs

We use the Gmail API for connecting your Gmail account to our service in order to manage support tickets. We also use the Shopify API for synchronizing your Shopify orders into our service. The usage of these APIs is in accordance with their respective terms of service.

Google Gmail API

Our service uses the Gmail API for improved customer service interactions. This integration allows our service to streamline and automate support communications for Shopify store owners. Specifically, here's how we use this API:

  • Email Access: We access incoming customer emails from the connected Gmail account. This allows our application to analyze the content of customer inquiries and generate appropriate responses. This data is accessed and processed in real time, and we do not store your emails.
  • Sending Emails: Our system sends emails on behalf of users to respond to customer inquiries. These email responses can be auto-generated by our AI model, or they can be drafted by support agents using suggested responses from the AI.
  • Conversation Context: By having access to email threads, our AI can understand the context of ongoing conversations. This improves the accuracy and relevancy of our AI's responses, enhancing the overall customer experience.

Shopify API

We integrate with Shopify API to enhance the functionality of our service. This allows us to provide a seamless and efficient customer support system for Shopify store owners. Specifically, here's how we use this API:

  • Order Data: We access and process order data from the connected Shopify store. This data is used to inform the AI model about order statuses, product information, and other order-related details, enabling it to provide accurate responses to customer inquiries related to orders.
  • Customer Data: We also access customer data to personalize and enhance the customer service experience. However, this data is processed in real time and is not stored on our servers.
  • Shop Management: With the Shopify API, our system can interact with shop management functions, which allows us to auto-generate responses that can guide customers on product inquiries, shipping details, and more.

Despite the extensive integration, we ensure that the Gmail & Shopify API is used strictly for the stated functionality. We respect user privacy and are committed to maintaining the security of user data. The data used by our application is exclusively used for improving the quality of customer service interactions and is not stored or used for any other purposes. We comply with all relevant privacy laws and regulations in relation to the use of the API.

5. Sharing of Personal Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your personal information with third parties in order to provide our services. These third parties are not allowed to use your personal information for any other purpose than to provide the service.

Our service employs OpenAI's ChatGPT to facilitate efficient and accurate customer service interactions. It's important to clarify that the use of ChatGPT takes place within the user's own environment, using the user's own OpenAI API key. As such, we as service providers do not have direct access to the data processed by OpenAI. Here's how we use this API:

  • Customer Inquiry Analysis: We use the AI model to analyze customer inquiries collected from the user's Gmail account and generate appropriate responses.
  • Order Information Processing: The AI model processes order data from the user's connected Shopify store to provide accurate and relevant responses to order-related inquiries.
  • Contextual Understanding: By processing email threads and order data, ChatGPT is able to understand the context of ongoing conversations and provide more accurate and relevant responses.

All data processed by the OpenAI API is done so in real time and is not stored on our servers or in the user's OpenAI account. The sole purpose of this data processing is to enhance customer service interactions.

Rest assured that the OpenAI API key provided by the user is used strictly for the stated functionality, and we are committed to maintaining its confidentiality and security. As with all third-party integrations, we comply with all relevant privacy laws and regulations.
